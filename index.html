Biography

I am Antonela Tommasel. I am currently a Researcher at CONICET , working in the Recommender Systems group at ISISTAN Research Institute in Tandil, Argentina. I am also a Teacher Assistant at UNICEN .

My main research interests include social computing applications of machine learning and recommender systems.

6+ years of research experience. 8+ years of teaching experience.
